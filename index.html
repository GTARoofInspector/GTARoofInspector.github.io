About Me

I am a drone enthusiast that has been flying drones for the last few years. My skills mainly specialize in Aerial Photography and Cinematography. My services are ideal for real estate (both residential and commercial) and building inspections. I also offer pre-purchase home inspections for roof condition, including associated gutter conditions. and can provide personalized video editing with your logo and message available. This is where my services come in to fill the void. With GTA Roof Inspector, my services will give you the reassurance your roof is in good condition or in need of repair without the need to risk yourself going up on the roof, or simply if you do not own a ladder or have access to one.

Services

My service offerings are simple. You will email me which package you would want and the location of the property you are looking to inspect. The property owner will be required to fill out a waiver of consent to give us permission to be on their property for the specified amount of time. My services are ideal for individuals or companies who are:

1. Residential properties who do not have access to a ladder or do not want to risk their own well-being
2. Real estate agents or companies looking for information on roof condition
3. Home inspection companies who do not want to go up on the roof

A report will be provided to you with pictures of the roof of the property from North, East, West and South facing direction. Additional information given if other packages purchased (including video).
